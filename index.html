Virtual Hackathon, 29-31 October 2021

Welcome to µHacks!

Students of all the world and all education levels are welcome to participate in this virtual hackathon from October 29th to October 31th.

With a team of up to 3 other friends, you will have 36 hours to create a project which helps people managing small enterprises, startups and entrepeneurs improve. Additionally, you will be able to participate in many wokshops and activities to rest while developing your world-changing idea!

We can’t wait to see your mind-blowing ideas rise! See you at µHacks!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any coding experience?

Not at all! We will running workshops that will allow you to build a project from scratch even with no prior experience.

Can I participate with a team?

Of course! The max team size is 4. Although you can participate alone, we believe that participating with a team is a fundamental part of the hackathon experience. However, if you do not have one, do not worry! We’ll be hosting team-formation activities.

Can I submit my project to multiple hackathons?

Sure! Hacks are for being shared, so there is no problem in submitting your projects to multiple hackathons. However, ensure that the other hackathon allows it as well.
