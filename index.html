µHacks

µprojects with BIG IMPACT in µenterprises

Virtual Hackathon, 21-23 January 2022

Welcome to µHacks!

Students of all the world and all education levels are welcome to participate in this virtual hackathon from January 21th to January 23th.

This year's theme is "µentreprises". With a team of up to 3 other friends, you will have 36 hours to create a project which helps people managing small enterprises, startups and entrepeneurs improve. They can be related directly to the economics part of the enterprise, to the well-being or productivity of their employees, etcetera, as long as the relation with µenterprises is clear.

This hackathons welcomes people both with and without previous coding experience. Additionally, you will be able to participate in many wokshops and activities to rest while developing your world-changing idea!

We can’t wait to see your mind-blowing ideas rise! See you at µHacks!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register?

Complete the registration form in the "Register me now!" button and then use the links there to join our Discord server. If you have trouble, contact us in contact@uhacks.org and we'll gladly help you.

How much does it cost?

Nothing. It's free!

Do I need any coding experience?

Not at all! We will running workshops that will allow you to build a project from scratch even with no prior experience.

Can I participate with a team?

Of course! The max team size is 4. Although you can participate alone, we believe that participating with a team is a fundamental part of the hackathon experience. However, if you do not have one, do not worry! We’ll be hosting team-formation activities.

Can I submit my project to multiple hackathons?

Sure! Hacks are for being shared, so there is no problem in submitting your projects to multiple hackathons. However, ensure that the other hackathon allows it as well.

I have other question.

No problem. Contact us at contact@uhacks.org or in ask your question in our Discord server and we'll answer you as fast as possible.
